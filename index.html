About / FAQ

How do I mint an eco-badge?

Join a quest, upload verifiable proof (photo, QR, or IoT), then press "Mint NFT". The app will call the mint API (Verbwire or your backend).

What is BlockDAG?

BlockDAG is the ledger tech used for fast micropayments and near-instant settlement in our design.
